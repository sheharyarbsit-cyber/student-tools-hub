How to Calculate CGPA

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is an important academic score used in schools, colleges, and universities. It represents your overall academic performance across all semesters.

To calculate CGPA, you need to convert your grades into grade points, multiply by credit hours, and divide by total credits. Our CGPA calculator makes this process quick and accurate.
